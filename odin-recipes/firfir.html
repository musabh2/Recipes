Firfir Recipe

Description

Firfir is a popular breakfast dish that originates from Ethiopia. The simplicity of the dish requiring very few ingredients, combined with its delicious taste makes it a vey popular dish in it's home country.

Ingredients

  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Oil
  • Berbere (Ethiopian Spice)
  • Injera (Ethiopian Bread)
  • Water

Steps

  1. Dice 2 onions and 2 tomatoes and half a garlic
  2. Heat the diced onion until the moisture from the onion dries up
  3. Add two spoons of your favourite oil
  4. Heat the mixture for about 5 minutes without burning the onion
  5. Add the tomatoes and garlic to the mixture
  6. Heat for 5 minutes
  7. Add two spoons of Berbere
  8. Heat for 5 minutes
  9. Heat the mixture well adding a little water to avoid burning the ingredients
  10. After about 10 minutes add around two cups of water
  11. Let it heat up for 3 minutes
  12. Turn off the heat and add the injera to the mixture
